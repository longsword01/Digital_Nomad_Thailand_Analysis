# Digital_Nomad_Thailand_Analysis
This project explores global digital nomad travel patterns, analyzing where nomads are based and which cities they visit most. Using Excel  and Tableau for visualization, the dashboard breaks down movement from a country level down to the most popular cities worldwide and within Thailand
## Tools Used
- Excel - data source
- Tableau — dashboard and visualizations
## Key Insights
- Top countries where digital nomads are based
- Most visited cities globally
- Most popular destinations within Thailand
## Files
- `tableau_thailand — download and open in Tableau to explore the full dashboard
## Conclusion
Thailand emerges as one of the top destinations for digital nomads 
globally, ranking as the most visited country outside of the United 
States. Within Thailand, Bangkok and Chiang Mai dominate as the 
preferred hubs, leading in both number of trips and total days stayed. 
This suggests that digital nomads are not just passing through Thailand 
— they are staying for extended periods, indicating strong appeal in 
terms of cost of living, infrastructure, and lifestyle.
